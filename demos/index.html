Grounded Q&A System

Ask questions about your documents and receive accurate, source-cited answers. Our RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) system grounds responses in your actual data, eliminating hallucinations.

How to use: Upload your documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT), then ask natural language questions. The system will retrieve relevant passages and generate answers with source citations.

Invoice Data Extraction

Automatically extract structured data from invoices with high accuracy. Our AI handles various invoice formats, layouts, and languages to capture vendor details, line items, totals, and dates.

How to use: Upload an invoice image or PDF. The system will automatically detect and extract key fields including invoice number, dates, amounts, vendor information, and line items.

Demand Forecasting

Predict future demand patterns using historical data and advanced time-series models. Our forecasting engine helps optimize inventory, reduce waste, and improve supply chain planning.

How to use: Upload historical sales or demand data (CSV format with dates and quantities). Specify your forecast horizon, and the model will generate predictions with confidence intervals.

Anomaly Detection

Identify unusual patterns and outliers in your data streams in real-time. Perfect for fraud detection, quality control, system monitoring, and predictive maintenance applications.

How to use: Upload your time-series or tabular data. The system will analyze patterns and flag anomalies with severity scores, helping you catch issues before they become problems.
